Servo Motor Calibration

Important Calibration Warning!

BEFORE YOU START:

  1. Disconnect all Servo-Horns from your Servo Motors first!
  2. This process will set the digital center (0) for each servo. If horns are attached, they might be forced into an extreme position, potentially damaging the servo or the robot.

After calibrating, attach the horns at their desired neutral (0 degree) position. Then, save the calibration.
